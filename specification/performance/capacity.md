# Capacity

Capacity is the maximum load the system must support — concurrent users, data volume, or other resource limits — without degradation below the specified [latency](./latency.md) and [throughput](./throughput.md) thresholds.

_State the capacity targets the system MUST meet. For example:_

- The system MUST support at least 10,000 concurrent authenticated users.

- The system MUST remain operational with a dataset of at least 100 million records without degradation of query performance.
