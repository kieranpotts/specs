# Mission statement

_A single, concise paragraph stating the core purpose of the system: who it serves, and the value it delivers. The mission statement should be stable — it changes rarely, and only in response to a fundamental shift in the product's direction._

The Petstore API exists to give pet retailers, marketplace operators, and their technology partners instant, reliable access to a curated catalog of pets and pet-related products. By exposing this information through a well-defined digital interface, we enable businesses to build rich shopping experiences and automate procurement workflows without maintaining their own catalog infrastructure.
